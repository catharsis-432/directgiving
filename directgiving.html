The Core Problem

60%

of crisis donations are unhelpful — CIDI / BBB Wise Giving Alliance

Generosity without guidance
creates a second disaster.

When donations flood in after a crisis, most are unsolicited and unusable — expired goods, wrong sizes, irrelevant items. Relief centers become overwhelmed while actual needs go unmet. DirectGiving fixes this.

👨‍👩‍👧

Families

Experience prolonged deprivation of essential goods — medicine, baby formula, food — while storage rooms fill with unwanted clutter.

🏢

NGOs & Social Workers

Face burnout managing "clutter donations," incur high storage and disposal costs, and lose precious time sorting unusable items.

🤝

Donors

Feel frustrated and distrustful when gifts don't visibly help anyone — reducing future donations and weakening the giving culture.

Platform Features

Built to make every
donation count.

Every feature is designed around one goal: zero-waste, maximum-impact giving.

🗺️

Dynamic Distribution Map

A live, hyper-local map showing all donation hubs, current need hotspots by area, and real-time fulfillment status across your region.

🤖

AI Quality Filter

Our AI screens every incoming donation before acceptance — preventing expired, damaged, or irrelevant goods from reaching relief centers and creating a "second disaster."

📋

Verified Live Wishlist

Only verified social workers and barangay leaders can post needs. A "Pull" logistics model means donors only give what's actually asked for — no guesswork.

🔒

Institutional Verification

Every NGO and community partner is authenticated through municipal social welfare agencies. Your trust is backed by government accountability.

📊

Real-Time Impact Reports

Donors receive transparent reports showing exactly who received their gifts. NGOs can share these reports to strengthen donor relationships and secure future support.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Built with React Native for iOS and Android, plus a full web dashboard. Donate, track, and manage from any device, anywhere — even in low-connectivity areas.
