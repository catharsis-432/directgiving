How It Works

Precision giving in
three simple steps.

Our AI-powered registry makes sure every donation counts — no guesswork, no waste.

📋

NGOs Post Real Needs

Verified social workers and community leaders log exactly what their families need right now — in real time — through our live wishlist dashboard.

🤖

AI Matches Your Donation

Our smart matching algorithm pairs your available items directly with the highest-priority, nearest community need — no middleman, no delay.

📍

You See the Impact

Track your donation on our Dynamic Distribution Map. Know exactly where your gift went and who it helped — full transparency, always.

Platform Features

Built to eliminate
donation waste.

Everything you need to give smart, give local, and give with confidence.

🗺️

Dynamic Distribution Map

A live, hyper-local map showing donation hubs, current needs by area, and real-time fulfillment status across your community.

✅

AI Quality Filter

Our AI-Powered Quality Filter screens donations before they're accepted — preventing the "Second Disaster" of unusable or expired goods reaching relief centers.

📱

Live Wishlist by Social Workers

Verified community leaders manage a real-time registry of exactly what's needed. Donors only see what's genuinely asked for — a "Pull" not "Push" model.

🔒

Verified Recipient Network

Every NGO and community partner is verified through municipal social welfare agencies. Your trust is protected by institutional accountability.
